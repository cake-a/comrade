Welcome, Comrade.

Our mission is to prepare students for higher education and ensure success for all students taking the financial risk for a degree.

Today, students are forced to gamble thousands of dollars towards a degree.

My hypothesis is, if today's students share their course pathways, booklists, and reading guides for their specific major and school,

then they can help increase the chance for success for future students by giving them a means to prepare prior to entering higher education institutions.

Learning a new subject in 3 months is not ideal for everyone.

Not everyone is talented, hardworking, or priveleged enough to have ample study time.

Our mission is to provide resources and an opportunity for others to prepare prior to purchasing a course, so nobody gets left behind.

Life doesn't have to be a competition. This can be a teamwork effort.

We live together in the same space and time, so please join us in being comrades toward higher success rates in the higher education.
