  • SUBMISSIONS

Welcome, Comrade.

Our mission is to prepare students for higher education and ensure higher rates of success for all students taking the financial risk towards learning.

Today, students are forced to gamble thousands of dollars towards a chance of earning a degree, and are expected to learn a new subject (and pass) in only a few months.

The reality is that not everyone has the means to take on this gamble.

Students are expected to be talented, hardworking, healthy, and priveleged enough to afford classes and have ample study time.

My hypothesis is, if today's students share their course pathways, booklists, and reading guides for their specific major and school,

then they can help increase the chance for success for future students by giving them the means to prepare prior to entering their courses.

Our mission is to provide resources and an opportunity for students to prepare prior to registering for their course, so nobody gets left behind.

So please join us in being comrades together toward higher success rates in higher education.
